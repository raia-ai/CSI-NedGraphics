NedGraphics Event Follow-Up _Example Heimtextil
Touchpoint 1: Connect at event through LinkedIn
Touchpoint 2: Call after event
Touchpoint 3 _Follow up Email1:
Hi [First Name],
It was such a pleasure meeting you at Heimtextil 2025! I really enjoyed seeing your vibrant colours and intricate floral patterns.
I’d love to continue our conversation and introduce you to my Sales Manager [name]. Together, we can explore how  NedGraphics'  solutions could help streamline your design process, reduce design time, and enhance collaboration.
Please let me know what works best for you:
A. I’m interested and available on [Day/Time].
B. Not a priority right now, but happy to stay in touch.
C. I’m not interested.
Looking forward to hearing from you!
Best regards,
Sabine
Touchpoint 5 – Follow up Call
Touchpoint 6: Send direct message on LinkedIn/Sales Navigator
Touchpoint 7: Added Value Email 2:
Hi [First Name],
I hope this email finds you well. I left you a message recently and completely understand this is a busy time for you.
I just wanted to follow up after Heimtextil and share something I believe you’ll find valuable.
Attached is a short success story (dependent on vertical) that highlights how our customers are benefiting from NedGraphics, including:
✅ Increased creativity
✅ Over 90% reduction in sampling costs and design errors
✅ Meaningful contributions to sustainability goals
✅ At least 50% faster design cycles
Would you be open to a quick call next week to walk through your workflow, discuss potential challenges, and explore where NedGraphics could add value? I’ll be happy to work around your schedule.
Warm regards,
Sabine
Touchpoint 8: Follow up Call
Touchpoint 9: Final Email Options
Hi [First Name],
I wanted to follow up one last time to see if this is of interest to you and your workflow, and whether it makes sense to connect about how NedGraphics could help streamline your design process.
If a call isn’t the right step now, you’re welcome to stay in touch through our updates and resources - we regularly share insights on trends, workflow efficiency, and design innovations that many of our partners find valuable.
To make it simple, please let me know what fits best:
A. I’m interested and available on [Day/Time].
B. Not a priority right now, but please add me to your newsletter to stay informed.
C. I’m not interested.
Wishing you continued success and looking forward to connecting with you again in the future.
Warm regards,
Sabine
