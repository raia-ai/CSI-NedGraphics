# Storyboard Cataloguing Pro

Comprehensive documentation for Storyboard & Cataloguing Pro including features, benefits, and usage guidelines for textile and apparel design professionals.

## Main Toolbar Functions

The main toolbar provides essential functions including Open, Project, Print, Copy, Modules, Redo, Redo All, and Help Mode.

**Print** - Prints your document using current settings.

**Print Preview** - Click to open print menu and preview layout before printing.

**Cut** - Click to remove the image of the active window and copy it to the clipboard.

**Copy** - Click to keep the image of the active window and copy it to the clipboard.

**Paste** - Click to paste the contents of the clipboard.

**Undo All** - Click to undo the series of actions since you last opened the file.

**Redo All** - Click to redo all actions altered in Undo.

**About** - Gives you the latest software data, including version numbers.

**Help Mode** - Full detailed guide about the software.

## Window Display Options

**Cascade** - Display all open windows in overlapping formation.

**Tile Horizontally** - Stacks the windows.

**Tile Vertically** - Sets windows side by side.

## Module Toolbar

Shows the modules currently accessible for linking:

**Load Scan** - Click to link to Color Reduction and Cleaning Pro.

**Cleaning** - Click to link to Color Reduction and Cleaning Pro.

**Designing** - Click to link to Design and Repeat Pro.

**Coloring** - Click to link to Easy Coloring Pro.

**Layout** - Click to link to Storyboard and Cataloguing Pro.

## Insert Objects Toolbar

Displays options to insert a picture, 3D, fabric, article, or material.

**Straight Line** - Draw a straight line.

**Insert Text** - Click and drag to create a text box.

**Apply Fabric** - Select Apply Fabric button and then click and drag a fabric onto an article or mapping.

**Mapping Origin** - Change where the origin of a fabric is positioned on an article or mapping.

**Open Browsers Manager** - Displays list of open books and allows you to add multiple books of the same type, from different file locations.

**Object Properties** - Displays object properties toolbar at bottom of page.

**Object Manager** - Displays object manager to left of window, which lists all elements of each page.

**Atlas Manager** - View and manage which atlases are open.

**Rulers** - Click to show rulers along side the board.

**Grid** - Display a grid to make layout-related operations easier.

## Drawing Tools

**Insert Object Easy Map** - Insert Easy Map 3DM file.

**Straight Line** - Click and drag to draw a straight line of various thickness in any direction or horizontal, vertical, or diagonal limitations.

**Rectangle** - Click and drag to draw squares or rectangles either filled or outlined.

**Ellipse** - Click and drag to draw circles or ellipses either filled or outlined.

## Object Grouping and Arrangement

**Group** - Select two objects or more and group them.

**Ungroup** - Ungroup a set of selected objects that were previously grouped.

**Ungroup all Page** - Ungroup all groups of objects on page.

**Change Position** - Displays Z-Order options to move objects in front or behind.

**Alignment Toolbar** - Displays Alignment options to line objects up horizontally or vertically.

**Distribution Toolbar** - Displays Distribution options to arrange three or more objects evenly along the x or y axis of the page.

**Arrange Objects in Page** - When selected, objects can be aligned to the parameters of the page rather than to each other.

**Window Arrangement Toolbar** - Arrange storyboard windows.

## Z-Order Controls

**Move to Background** - Moves your current motif to the back.

**Move Behind** - Moves your current motif behind another motif.

**Move in Front** - Moves your current motif on top of another motif.

**Move to Foreground** - Moves your current motif all the way to the front.

## Alignment Options

**Align Left Edge** - Aligns left edges of selected objects to each other or to left edge of the page.

**Align Centered Vertically** - Click to align objects vertically, according to their centers.

**Align Right Edge** - Aligns right edges of selected objects to each other or to right edge of the page.

**Align Top** - Aligns top edges of selected objects to each other or to top edge of the page.

**Align Centered Horizontally** - Align objects horizontally, according to their centers.

**Align Bottom** - Align bottom edges of selected objects to each other or to bottom edge of the page.

## Distribution Tools

When three objects are selected, the distribution tools arrange them evenly on the page:

**Distribute from Top** - Distributes three objects evenly from top of page with respect to top edges.

**Distribute Horizontally from Center** - Distributes three objects evenly from the centre of page.

**Distribute from Bottom** - Distributes three objects evenly from bottom of page with respect to bottom edges.

**Distribute from Left** - Distributes three objects evenly from left of page with respect to left edges.

**Distribute Vertically from Center** - Distributes three objects evenly from the centre of page.

**Distribute from Right** - Distributes three objects evenly from right of page with respect to right edges.

## Book Management

**Open Picture Book** - Show picture book to insert a new picture.

**Open Easy Map Book** - Show 3D Book to insert objects with a 3DM extension.

**Open Article Book** - Show article book to insert a new article.

**Open 3D Book** - Open CDS Book - Show the Corel draw Sketches Book to insert a CDS file.

## Object Transformation

**Rotate 90 Left** - Rotate Object 90 degrees to the left.

**Rotate 90 Right** - Rotate Object 90 degrees to the right.

**Free Rotation** - Rotate by clicking and dragging object.

**Skew Object** - Skew object to a new shape.

**Crop Object** - Eliminate areas of a selected object.

**Restore Object** - Restore a rotated or skewed object to its original shape and position in order to edit it.

## Page Navigation

Provides the functionality to easily navigate through your pages sequence:

**New Page** - Add a page to the end of the presentation.

**Insert Page** - Add a page to a chosen location within the presentation.

**Duplicate Page** - Create a duplicate page and add to presentation.

**Delete Page** - Delete the page from the presentation.

**Page Properties** - Opens the object properties dialog.

**Formatting Page** - Takes you to the formatting page of the presentation, where any changes made will be applied to all pages of the presentation.

**Go to Page** - Allows you to navigate to any page of the presentation.

**Move Page** - Opens the Move page dialog where you can rearrange pages.

## Color Management

**Search in Multiple Atlases** - Search colors by parts of their name or color info to display a list of possible matches. Click a name from a list to display the color.

**Color Atlas** - Shows the color atlases that have been opened in the atlas manager.

## Article Management

Right click on an article for various options.

Double click an article to color block it for different colors or fabrics.
