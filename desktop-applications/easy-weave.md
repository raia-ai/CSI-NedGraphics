Functions Toolbar
1. Warp Repeat - click to set a single repeat size in the warp fringe in the end location of
   your design
2. Warp Repetition - click to set a repeat tab in the warp
3. Warp Symmetry - click to set a symmetry tab in the center area of the warp
4. Automatic Copy - keep selected to draw exactly the same stripes in the warp and in the
   weft.
5. Weft Repeat - click to set the repeat size in the weft fringe in the end location of your
   design
6. Weft Repetition - click to set a repeat tab in the weft
7. Weft Symmetry - allows to set a symmetry tab in the center area of the weft

          Editing Toolbar
          1. Draw Stripes - click to draw stripes in fringes to create your design
          2. Stripe Deletion - click to delete stripes from fringes
          3. Stripe Rotation - click to change the starting point of the design.
          4. Stripe Resize - click and drag to increase or decrease size of stripe.
          5. Select Color - click on the fabric with the eyedropper to select one of its colors
          6. Change Color - click on the fabric to select and modify one of its colors
          7. Copy Warp to Weft - click to copy the warp pattern created, to the weft
          8. Copy Weft to Warp - click to copy the weft pattern created, to the warp
          9. Swap Warp and Weft
          10. Select/Copy tool - click and drag an area in the fringe to select and copy a part of the
              pattern and move it to a new placement.
          11. Select/Copy by color - click on a color in the fringe in the fringe to select and copy a part
              of the pattern and move it to a new placement.
          12. Mirror - click to mirror the selected sequence.
          13. Fill stripes - click to fill a stripe with the currently selected color in the palette.
          14. Fill Identical Stripes- all stripes with the same color will automatically update.
          15. Atlas Manager – View and manage list to linked atlases.
          16. Coloring Assistant
          17. Simulate - click this button to show a woven simulation
          18. 3D View - allows you to view the current weaving project mapped onto one of the
              predefined 3D images having been supplied with the application
          19. Tracing - click on icon to open a file to trace
          20. Select Tracing Color – Use to select colors from the tracing to your palette.
          21. Change Origin Warp/Weft - click and drag to change the starting point of the tracing
          22. Adjust Density Warp/Weft – click and drag on warp or weft to visually change density.
